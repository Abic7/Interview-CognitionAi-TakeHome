Key Decisions & Rationale

Why we made specific choices that shaped the system.

Devin IS the system. The orchestrator's only job is send task + watch results. Complex orchestration adds no value and increases failure modes. 150 lines of clean Python is all we need.

Webhooks require public endpoints (ngrok complexity). Polling is simple, works locally, and 30-second latency is fine for this use case. Real-time isn't needed for issues that took weeks to remediate.

Single-instance orchestrator doesn't need distributed database. SQLite is lightweight, persistent, observable, and Docker-friendly. Perfect for this problem scope.

2 issues guarantee success. 5+ risks partial work. We're proving the concept works end-to-end, not maximizing issue count. Quality > quantity.
